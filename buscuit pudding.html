Busicuit Pudding Recipe

Ingredients:

MILKMAID 390g 1 Tin

Margarine 200g

Cocoa powder 5 tbsp

Biscuits 60

How to Make Busicuit Pudding

1. Mix 330g MILKMAID, margarine, and cocoa powder to make the chocolate paste.

2. Add 60ml MILKMAID to 40ml water in a separate bowl and mix. Dip biscuits in the MILKMAID mix and place them at the bottom of a glass dish. Spread the chocolate paste over the biscuit layer in the dish and repeat.

3. Garnish with cashew (optional) and refrigerate for 4 hours.
