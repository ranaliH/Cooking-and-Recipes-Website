Chicken Biriyani Recipe

Ingredients:

600gChicken

100gCurd / Yogurt

1tbspGinger and garlic paste

2tbspCoriander powder

2tbspGaram masala

2tbspChili powder

1tspTurmeric powder

1tspCumin powder

1tspPepper powder

2tspSalt

1cubeMAGGI Chicken Seasoning Cube

2tbspfresh lemon juice

3tbsp Oil

1tbsp Ginger and garlic paste

2oz Star Anise

2Leafs Bay

3Mediums Cardamom

3oz cloves

1Stick Cinnamon

400g Onion

500g Tomato

2 tbsp Chili powder

0.5 tsp Turmeric powder

2 tbsp Coriander powder

1 tsp Garam masala

0.5 tsp Cumin powder

25g Mint Leaves

25g Coriander leaves

0.5 tbspRose Water

600g Cooked Ghee Rice

How to Make

1. Marinate the chicken using all the ingredients mentioned for marination .

2. Place a pot on a high heat stove with oil.

3. Add 1 tbsp of ginger / garlic paste into this.

4. Add in the Star anise, bay leaves, cardamom, cloves and cinnamon stick into this.

5. When golden brown; add in chopped onions and diced tomatoes.

6. Whilst mixing add all the spices mentioned for the rice (chilli powder, coriander powder, Cumin powder, Garam Masala powder and Turmeric).

7. Add the marinated chicken, mint leaves, coriander leaves .

8. Close the lid and let the chicken cook in medium flame for about 10 minutes.

9. When the chicken is cooked add the cooked ghee rice into it.

10. Pour in ½ tbsp rose water and add few drops of orange food coloring. 11th Step

11. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves and fried onions few and close the lid.

12. Keep for 15 minutes at low flame

13. Garnish with fried onion, cashew nuts, coriander leaves and serve
