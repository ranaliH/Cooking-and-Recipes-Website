Garlic Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

1 pound Italian loaf or French loaf

1/2 cup softened unsalted butter (can also use salted)

4 cloves garlic, finely minced

1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley

1/8 teaspoon sea salt

1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese or 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

1/2tsp red chili powder

1/4tsp garam masala or coriander and cumin powder

For garnishing:

Extra parsley and chives

How to Make Garlic Bread

1. Prep your pan & bread.

2. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or tinfoil, slice your bread in half lengthwise, and place the bread cut side up on the baking sheet.

Make the garlic butter

3.In a small bowl mix the herbs and salt together with the softened butter.

4. Spread it evenly over the top of the bread halves.

Bake

5. Bake the bread for 10-15 minutes at 425 degrees F until it’s slightly golden brown on the edges. If you’re adding cheese, add it in the last 2 minutes of baking.

6. Then cool, slice into 1-inch pieces, and devour!

Don’t forget to dip it in this AMAZING Garlic Olive Oil Dip, or your favorite marinara sauce!If you like crispier garlic bread, bake it for 3-5 minutes more (but watch it carefully so that it doesn’t get too toasted). If you like it softer, take it out closer to 10 minutes. You can also wrap the bread in aluminum foil before baking it if you like softer garlic bread.
